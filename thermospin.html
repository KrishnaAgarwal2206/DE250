⚡ The Energy Chain

HOW IT WORKS

Your hand crank sets off a chain of energy transformations — watch mechanical effort become temperature you can feel.

01
Step 1 — Input
MECHANICAL ENERGY
You rotate the hand crank — this is Work (W). Your muscle energy is the only power source. No batteries. No plug. Just you.
02
Step 2 — Conversion
KINETIC → ELECTRICAL
A geared DC motor acts as a generator. The internal gear ratio amplifies your slow cranking into high-RPM rotation, producing usable DC voltage.
03
Step 3 — Control
ELECTRICAL CONTROL
Current passes through a DPDT switch — your choice of "Heat" or "Cool" determines the polarity of current entering the Peltier. Same electricity, opposite results.
04
Step 4 — Peltier Effect
ELECTRICAL → THERMAL
Current enters the Peltier module (TEC). Due to the Peltier Effect, heat is absorbed on one face and emitted on the other. Flip the polarity, flip the direction of heat flow.
05
Step 5 — You Feel It
THERMAL TRANSFER
The aluminium top plate becomes noticeably hot or cold. An internal fan keeps the heatsink at room temperature so the effect stays strong. You feel the science — no instruments needed.

📚 Curriculum Corner

THE SCIENCE

Two fundamental laws of thermodynamics — made tangible.

⚡
FIRST LAW

Energy is conserved — never created, never destroyed. When you crank, your muscle work (W) is converted into electrical energy, which becomes heat. The energy you put in must equal the energy that comes out.

Qh = Qc + Welec
❄️
SECOND LAW

Heat naturally flows from hot to cold. The Peltier module defies this by using external work (your cranking) to pump heat from cold to hot — just like a refrigerator, but powered by your arm!

COP = Qc / W

📖 User Guide

HOW TO USE IT

Five steps to experience thermodynamics with your own hands.

🔥
STEP 01
Flip to HOT
Turn the 3-position knob to HOT. You're setting which direction heat will be pumped.
⚙️
STEP 02
Crank Steadily
Rotate at a steady pace. Each turn spins the internal motor — you ARE the power station!
🖐️
STEP 03
Touch the Plate
Place a finger on the top metal plate. Within 15–30 seconds, you'll feel your energy become heat.
🔄
STEP 04
Switch to COLD
Without stopping, flip the switch to COLD. Same cranking — completely opposite result!
❄️
STEP 05
Feel the Cold
The same plate now pulls heat away from your finger. You just reversed the laws of natural heat flow!
CALRIC SAYS
Pro tip: Keep cranking at a steady pace - the faster and more consistent you are, the stronger the effect! Also, make sure to feel the plate before you start cranking so you notice the temperature change. Science is all about comparison! And don't forget - the fan inside is always running in the same direction regardless of HOT or COLD mode. It keeps the heatsink cool so the Peltier always has a temperature difference to work with. 🔥❄️
